Our Story

Founded on a Mission to Restore Movement

Gopal Ortho was established in Nagercoil with a single purpose: to provide world-class orthopaedic care to the people of South Tamil Nadu without requiring them to travel to Chennai or abroad.

Our team of board-certified specialists brings sub-speciality training in joint replacement, spine surgery, sports medicine, and physiotherapy under one roof in Kottar.

We invest continuously in the latest surgical technology and clinical research to ensure our patients receive outcomes on par with leading international centres.
