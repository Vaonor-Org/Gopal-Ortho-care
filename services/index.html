Specialized Services

Complete orthopaedic and rehabilitation care

From urgent trauma to planned surgery, we follow a step-by-step care pathway that supports safe treatment, faster recovery, and lasting results.

01 - Orthopaedic

Fracture and Trauma Care

Urgent assessment and definitive care for fractures and orthopaedic trauma, with safe surgery and coordinated rehabilitation.

Who needs this

Patients with fractures, dislocations, accident injuries, or pain and swelling after a fall.

Symptoms and conditions

Closed or open fractures, joint dislocations, periarticular fractures, and delayed union.

Benefits

Stable fixation, reduced pain, proper alignment, and faster return to daily activity.

Technology and techniques

Image-guided reduction, locking plates, intramedullary nails, and minimally invasive fixation when suitable.

Recovery

Early mobilization with physiotherapy. Healing timelines vary by bone and overall health.

02 - Orthopaedic

Joint Replacement

Advanced knee and hip replacement using proven implants and structured recovery. For Knee Replacement in Nagercoil, our team provides end-to-end surgical and rehab care.

Who needs this

Severe arthritis pain, deformity, or limited walking despite medicines and therapy.

Symptoms and conditions

Osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, avascular necrosis, and post-traumatic arthritis.

Benefits

Long-term pain relief, improved walking distance, and better alignment.

Technology and techniques

Pre-op planning, cemented or cementless implants, and precise alignment tools.

Recovery

Most patients start walking with support within 24 to 48 hours. Full recovery varies over weeks to months.

03 - Orthopaedic

Spine Surgery

Comprehensive spine care led by a Spine Surgery Specialist Nagercoil patients trust, from diagnosis to minimally invasive surgery and rehab.

Who needs this

Persistent back or neck pain with nerve symptoms, weakness, or limited function.

Symptoms and conditions

Disc prolapse, spinal stenosis, spondylolisthesis, cervical myelopathy, and deformity.

Benefits

Relief from nerve pain, improved stability, and safer daily movement.

Technology and techniques

Microsurgery, minimally invasive decompression, fusion when needed, and image guidance.

Recovery

Short hospital stay, brace if required, and guided physiotherapy for safe return to activity.

04 - Sports Medicine

Sports Medicine

Injury-specific care for athletes and active adults, from diagnosis to return-to-sport testing.

Who needs this

ACL or PCL injuries, meniscus tears, shoulder instability, and tendon pain.

Symptoms and conditions

Ligament tears, meniscus injuries, rotator cuff tears, and cartilage defects.

Benefits

Restores joint stability, reduces swelling, and protects long-term joint health.

Technology and techniques

Arthroscopic repair, ligament reconstruction, and sports rehab protocols.

Recovery

Phased rehabilitation. Return to sport is based on strength and control testing.

05 - Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation

Personalized rehabilitation at our Physiotherapy Center Nagercoil patients rely on for pain relief, mobility, and recovery.

Who needs this

Post-surgical patients, chronic pain, stiffness, or neurological recovery needs.

Symptoms and conditions

Back and neck pain, frozen shoulder, knee arthritis, and post-op weakness.

Benefits

Improves strength, reduces pain, restores balance, and prevents re-injury.

Technology and techniques

Manual therapy, electrotherapy, gait training, and home exercise plans.

Recovery

Progress is tracked weekly with measurable goals and tailored programs.

06 - Pain Management

Pain Management

Non-surgical options to control chronic musculoskeletal pain and improve daily function.

Who needs this

Chronic back or joint pain, persistent tendon pain, and nerve irritation.

Symptoms and conditions

Facet joint pain, sciatica, arthritis, tendonitis, and bursitis.

Benefits

Reduces pain, improves sleep and activity, and may delay or avoid surgery.

Technology and techniques

Targeted injections, PRP therapy, medication optimization, and physiotherapy.

Recovery

Plans are individualized. Many patients see gradual improvement over weeks.

FAQs

Answers to common patient questions

If you have more questions, our team is ready to help.

When should I see an orthopaedic specialist?

See a specialist if pain lasts more than two weeks, limits movement, or follows an injury or fall.

Do I need surgery for knee arthritis?

Not always. We start with medicines, injections, and physiotherapy. Surgery is suggested only when pain severely affects daily life.

How long does knee replacement recovery take?

Most patients walk with support in 24 to 48 hours. Full recovery depends on health and rehab, usually over weeks to months.

Is spine surgery always required for back pain?

No. Many cases improve with therapy and lifestyle changes. Surgery is recommended only for nerve compression or severe instability.

What happens during the first consultation?

We review symptoms, examine you, and recommend tests if needed. You leave with a clear plan and expected timeline.

Do you provide physiotherapy after surgery?

Yes. Rehabilitation starts early and continues with supervised sessions and home exercises.

Can I get a second opinion?

Absolutely. We encourage patients to feel confident and informed about their treatment plan.

Do you handle emergency fractures at night?

Yes. Our 24/7 trauma care team is available for urgent fracture management and stabilization.
