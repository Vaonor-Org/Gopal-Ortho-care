About Gopal Ortho

15 Years of Precision
Orthopaedic Care

Founded with the mission to bring world-class musculoskeletal care to South Tamil Nadu, Gopal Ortho Care Hospital has grown into the region's most trusted orthopaedic institution — without patients needing to travel to Chennai or beyond.

  • Board-certified specialists with sub-specialty fellowships in joint replacement, spine, and sports medicine
  • State-of-the-art C-arm imaging, arthroscopy, and minimally invasive surgical suites
  • Comprehensive in-house physiotherapy and rehabilitation with personalised care plans
  • 24/7 emergency orthopaedic care with rapid response trauma management protocols
